Well Still Life (A story about collected objects)
● I discover a unique esthetic sense from the crude appearances of my private collection which started with a personal taste.
This is meant to show sceneries of our daily lifes which are reflected as personal desires and social phenomenon in other words 
details of objects which change along time symptoms. ■ CHAYOUNGSEOK
